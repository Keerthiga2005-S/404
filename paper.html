Types of Paper

Explore different types of paper and their uses

Glossy Paper

Glossy paper is coated to provide a shiny finish, ideal for high-quality photographs and vibrant prints.

Matte Paper

Matte paper offers a non-reflective surface, making it perfect for professional documents and art prints.

Recycled Paper

Recycled paper is made from post-consumer waste, providing an eco-friendly option for everyday printing and writing.
